Grandma's (Quick) Pork in the Oven

Home

Description

A quick and simple recipe Grandma passed down during my early college years. It's quick, hands off, and tasty; bringing fond memories and a sense of comfort.

Ingrediants

  • Pork of some kind
  • Lawry's seasoned salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning mix

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees; 30 minutes bake time
  2. Season pork on both sides and wrap in tinfoil, with the fat side facing up
  3. For the final five minutes of baking, quickly take out pork, insert 1/2 Tbsp butter into foil, re-wrap, and return to oven for final ~5 minutes
