Description

A relatively quick chicken bake recipe for boneless/skinless breasts. Juicy and flavorful, mayonnaise and parmesan cheese are highlighted for a unique but scrumptious combination

Ingrediants

  • 1lb Chicken Breasts, diced
  • 3/4 cup mayo/greek yogurt
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 tsp seasoned salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Grease 9 X 13 baking pan and place diced chicken on the bottom
  2. In small bowl, combine mayo, parmesan chees, seasoned salt, pepper, garlic, and red pepper flakes
  3. Spread mixture evenly on top of chicken
  4. Sprinkle bread crumbs evenly on top of chicken
  5. Place pan in preheated oven, 375 degrees, bake for approx. 30-35 minutes, or until chkn is cooked through
